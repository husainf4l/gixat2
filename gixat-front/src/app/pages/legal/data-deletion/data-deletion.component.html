Your Right to Data Deletion

At Gixat, we respect your right to control your personal data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your personal information and what happens when you make such a request.

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time, subject to certain legal and operational requirements outlined below.

What Data Can Be Deleted

You can request deletion of the following types of personal data:

  • Account information (name, email, phone number, profile picture)
  • Organization information (if you are the owner)
  • Customer records and contact information
  • Vehicle information and service history
  • Job cards and service records
  • Appointment and session data
  • Uploaded documents and media files
  • Communication history and messages

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request data deletion through the following methods:

Method 1: Through Your Account

  1. Log in to your Gixat account
  2. Navigate to your account settings
  3. Select "Delete Account" or "Request Data Deletion"
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request

Method 2: Contact Us Directly

Send an email to privacy@gixat.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your full name and email address associated with your account
  • A clear statement that you wish to delete your data
  • Verification of your identity (we may request additional information)

Processing Time

Once we receive and verify your deletion request, we will process it within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

In some cases, deletion may take longer due to the complexity of the request or technical requirements. We will notify you if additional time is needed.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained even after your deletion request, including:

  • Legal Obligations: Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements
  • Financial Records: Transaction and billing records required for accounting and tax purposes
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated or anonymized data that cannot be used to identify you
  • Backup Systems: Data in backup systems may be retained until the backup is overwritten (typically 90 days)
  • Shared Data: Information shared with other users (e.g., in team accounts) may remain accessible to them

Account Deletion vs. Partial Data Deletion

You have two options when requesting data deletion:

Complete Account Deletion

This permanently deletes your account and all associated data. You will lose access to all services and cannot recover your account or data after deletion.

Partial Data Deletion

You can request deletion of specific data types while keeping your account active. For example, you may delete customer records but retain your account settings.

Consequences of Data Deletion

Please be aware that deleting your data may have the following consequences:

  • You will lose access to all services and features associated with your account
  • Historical data, reports, and analytics will no longer be available
  • You will not be able to recover deleted data
  • If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled
  • You may need to create a new account if you wish to use our services again

Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in our Privacy Policy. Generally:

  • Active accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Most data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backup data: May be retained for up to 90 days in backup systems
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Third-Party Services

If you have integrated third-party services with your Gixat account, you may need to separately request data deletion from those services. We are not responsible for data stored by third-party services.

Identity Verification

To protect your privacy and security, we may require verification of your identity before processing a deletion request. This may include confirming your email address, answering security questions, or providing additional documentation.

Contact Us

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance with your request, please contact us:

Email: privacy@gixat.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
